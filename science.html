Machine learning for science

Machine learning that shapes science.

Areas:

  • Computational chemistry: Exploring the molecular space and interactions.
  • Quantum machine learning: Exploring the interplay between quantum computing and machine learning.
  • Materials science: Exploring faster ways to compute materials properties and generate new kinds of materials.
  • Scientific knowledge a representation, reasoning and NLP: Exploring the knowledge representation and reasoning in science, esp. how reasoning is shaping science and how to (semi-)automate the process. Studying NLP problems in scientific literature, including information extraction, knowledge base construction, creation and difussion of ideas.
