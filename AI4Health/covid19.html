Covid-19 Archive

Forecasting, modelling, and public-health response work during the pandemic.

This archive captures a period of Covid-19 modelling and response work spanning forecasting, policy reporting, mobility estimation, and health-system analysis.

Program aims
  • Understand the dynamics of the pandemic
  • Support response through modelling and AI
  • Provide actionable public-health insight where possible

Forecasting across regions

Country and regional forecasting work included Australia, Brazil, Colombia, India, Peru, Russia, Spain, the USA, Vietnam, and global modelling views.

Drug repurposing and mobility

Additional work explored repurposing directions and mobility estimation from public online data sources.

Health systems response

The work sat within the broader AI for Health agenda, connecting forecasting with operational and policy questions.
