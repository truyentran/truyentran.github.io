One-sided filter bank for feature engineering in Electronic Medical Records

Medical records are noisy, episodic and irregular and thus creating great challenges for applying analytical tools. We invented a signal processing style method for extracting comprehensive features for any analysis tasks. [Published in: KDD'13, Journal under submission]. Applications in suicide risk prediction [Published in: KDD'13, KAIS, BMC Psychiatry]; cancer prognosis; heart attack prognosis [Published in: Aus Health Review]; heart failure prognosis [Published in:  IEEE-JBHI]; preterm bith prediction.
