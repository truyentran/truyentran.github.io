 Artificial Intelligence for Science

This research program aims to develop AI to automate the expensive discovery loop and bring science into AI models. 

 Aims
  • To understand God's plan for nature
  • To accelerate scientific discovery using AI
  • To advance AI that thinks and acts scientifically

AI Scientists

AI agents that imagine, think, plan, execute, and communicate like scientists. These capacities rest on goal-driven exploration of vast design spaces. Scientist agents work in team with human-in-the-loop to generate hypotheses, take advices, rationalize choices and explain the science. They are capable of communicating naturally with humans.

Publications

Physical Sciences

The goal is to understand God's plan for nature to speed up discovery. Examples include replacing hard physical computation and experiments such as computing quantum chemical properties, predicting molecular properties & interactions, predicting chemical reactions, planning for optimal synthesis pathways, understanding the structure and characteristics of materials, searching for new alloys, and generating molecules & crystals. Important areas include energy storage and carbon capturing. The broader picture includes green energy, climate change, and sustainability.

Publications

Life Sciences

The goal is to understand God's plan for living things. We want to unlock the mystery of life hidden under our genome, cells, and organisms. We will develop new AI to accelerate the finding of new drugs for new targets.
